Beneficiary app flow
Step 1
OTP login via mobile number
No username or password. A 6-digit OTP is sent to the registered mobile number — works for anyone, regardless of literacy.
Step 2
View pre-filled loan details
Bank or agency enters data beforehand. Beneficiary sees scheme name, loan amount, disbursement date, and declared asset type — nothing to fill in.
Step 3
Capture geo-tagged photo or video
App embeds GPS coordinates and timestamp into the image metadata automatically. Works offline — submission queued and synced when connectivity returns.
Step 4
On-device AI validates the asset
A lightweight image classifier (MobileNet-class) checks whether the photo matches the declared asset type. Result shown to beneficiary immediately.
Step 5
Submit to officer review queue
Submission held locally if offline. Auto-uploads on next sync. Officer notified automatically.
Step 6
SMS notification on outcome
Approved, re-upload requested, or escalated — all communicated via SMS so beneficiaries without smartphones can follow up too.
